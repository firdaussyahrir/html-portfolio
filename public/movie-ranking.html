The Best Movies According to Daus

My top 3 movies of all-time:

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A man is sentenced to life in prison for a crime he didn't commit and finds hope and friendship behind bars.

Inception (2010)

A team of thieves enters people’s dreams to steal information, but the mission becomes complicated as dreams and reality blur.

Parasite (2019)

A poor family infiltrates a wealthy family’s life through deceit, leading to unexpected chaos.
